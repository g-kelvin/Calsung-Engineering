Our Vision & Mission


Value Chain

Calsung Eng. Solutions Ltd has got several accounts with various manufacturers and wholesalers of water pumps, solar products, we also rely on importation of goods not locally manufactured.

Vision
To be one of the leading water, energy and Industrial system suppliers, contractors in Africa by 2030.

Mission
Our mission is to provide electrical,Water and mechanical solutions to our customers in Kenya and the region through diversification into manufacture of electrical & solar, water treatment, borehole products, project undertaking and management.

  • We will pursue this mission by the use of technology and dedicated personnel with the objective of realizing return and sustainable growth.
  • revising training programs for our personnel, keeping abreast with latest technology and upholding national laws.
  • We will promote the safe use of green energy & clean water in the community by offering quality and safe products and encouraging strict adherence to safety regulations both within our personnel and the community we serve.
