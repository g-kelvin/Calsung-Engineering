GENERAL STATEMENT

REVIEW PROCEDURES

This policy will be reviewed at least annually and any changes will be notified to all employees.

  • Calsung Eng. Solutions Ltd aims to achieve he highest possible standards of awareness of and attention to matters of safety, health and welfare at all levels.
  • Calsung Eng. Solutions Ltd will endeavour t all times to achieve standards that will exceed the minimum legal requirements.

SAFETY ORGANISATION

  • Calsung Eng.Sol Ltd will establish and maintain a structure of responsibility for safety and health matters through out the organization’s premises.
  • Calsung Eng. Sol Ltd will appoint the technical Director/ Quality control manager to act as a co-ordinator of safety and health matters.
  • The Technical Director/ Quality control manager will provide all employees with procedures for efficient and safe working environment.
  • Calsung Eng. Sol Ltd will provide all necessary information, training and supervision in safety and health matters to all employees
  • Calsung Eng. sol Ltd’s management will encourage the work of employees’ safety representatives.
    • INDIVIDUAL RESPONSIBILITIES
    Calsung Eng.Solutions Ltd will impress on every employee the need to be aware of their individual responsibility for taking due care at work and of co – operating with management in the implementation of this policy.
